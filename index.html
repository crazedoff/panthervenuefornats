BRHS and PantherVenue

The Bridgewater-Raritan High School, established in 1957, plays a vital role in Somerset County of New Jersey. The PantherVenue, our premier gymnasium, seats up to 3,500 and boasts modern amenities, including a high-quality sound system, adjustable lighting, and a large video screen. It was renovated in 2024, and features updated bleachers, a resurfaced floor, and new scoreboards, making it the ideal choice for sports, graduations, and community events.

Policies

Important rules and guidelines for using PantherVenue.

  • General
  • Tickets

Please review these policies to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience at PantherVenue.

No Outside Food

Outside food and beverages are prohibited to maintain cleanliness and support venue concessions.

Bag Checks

All bags are subject to search upon entry for safety and security purposes to ensure a safe environment.

No Smoking

Smoking, including e-cigarettes, is strictly prohibited inside the venue to maintain air quality.

Quiet Zones

Designated quiet zones are available for guests who wish to relax or have a private conversation.

Alcohol

Alcoholic beverages may not be consumed at PantherVenue. Doing so will result in immediate removal.

Pet Policy

Pets are not allowed on the premises, with the exception of service animals.

Parking

Parking is available in designated lots. Parking fees apply depending on the event.

Lost & Found

Items lost during an event can be found at the lost and found desk located near the main entrance.

Non-Refundable

Tickets are non-refundable unless the event is canceled by the venue, in which case full refunds will be issued.

Purchase Limit

A maximum of 10 tickets can be purchased per transaction to ensure fair distribution.

Lost Tickets

Lost or stolen tickets can be replaced at the box office with valid ID, but be sure to report it immediately.

Transferable

Tickets are transferable, but you must notify the box office of any changes in ownership prior to the event.

Special Discounts

Special discounts may be available for students and military personnel. Valid ID is required for verification.

VIP Tickets

VIP tickets offer additional perks such as early entry, exclusive seating, and access to VIP-only areas.

Group Tickets

Group ticket packages are available for parties of 10 or more at discounted rates. Please contact support.

Cancellations

If an event is canceled, ticket holders will receive refunds or the option to exchange tickets for future events.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PantherVenue

  • What is the purpose of this website?

    This platform serves as the official hub for PantherVenue - our 3,500-seat multi-purpose arena. Features include event listings, ticket purchases, venue rentals, photo galleries, and secure user accounts.

  • How do I purchase tickets for an event?

    1. Navigate to the Events section
    2. Select your desired event
    3. Choose seats using our interactive map
    4. Complete secure checkout
    Confirmations are sent via email/SMS

  • Can I rent the venue for an event?

    Yes! Our Rent Venue section provides:
    - Real-time availability calendar
    - Pricing calculator
    - Virtual tour
    - Instant booking requests
    Contact our events team for custom arrangements.

  • Is this website mobile-friendly?

    Absolutely! Features include:
    - Responsive design for all devices
    - Mobile-optimized checkout
    - Progressive Web App support
    - Dark mode compatibility

  • How can I find directions to the gymnasium?

    Use our interactive map with:
    - Real-time traffic updates
    - Parking recommendations
    - Public transport routes
    - 3D building visualization
    Three main entrances are clearly marked.

  • What are the seating options in the arena?

    We offer:
    - VIP Premium Seating
    - General Admission
    - Accessible seating
    - Group seating blocks
    View detailed charts in the About section.

  • Is my data secure when purchasing tickets?

    Your security is our priority:
    - PCI-DSS compliant payments
    - AES-256 encryption
    - Regular security audits
    - GDPR-compliant data handling

  • How do I subscribe to the newsletter?

    Subscribe through:
    1. Homepage subscription box
    2. Account dashboard
    3. Event confirmation emails
    Manage preferences anytime in your profile.

  • How do I know if an event is sold out?

    Look for:
    - "SOLD OUT" badge on event cards
    - Grayed-out seating options
    - Waitlist availability notifications
    Sign up for restock alerts in your account.

  • What are the arena policies?

    Key policies include:
    - Clear bag policy
